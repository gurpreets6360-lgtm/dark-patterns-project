Common Dark Patterns

Confirmshaming

Guilt-based manipulation

Newsletter Subscription

🔍 Analysis: The second option uses guilt ("don't want to save money") to pressure users into subscribing.

Impact: Users feel ashamed to opt out, leading to unwanted subscriptions.

✅ Solution: Use neutral language like "No thanks" or "Unsubscribe."

Roach Motel

Easy in, hard out

Cancel Premium Membership

Step 1: Click "Account Settings" → Hidden in dropdown
Step 2: Scroll to bottom → "Cancel Subscription" in grey text
Step 3: Click through 3 confirmation screens
Step 4: "We're sad to see you go!" with guilt message

Impact: Users give up trying to cancel, leading to unwanted charges.

✅ Solution: One-click cancellation with clear, accessible options.

Nagging

Persistent interruptions

📱 Enable notifications? Get instant updates!

Impact: Repeated requests frustrate users into compliance.

✅ Solution: Respect user choices with a "Don't ask again" option.

✨ Ethical Design Principles

🔓 Clear Choices

Options presented equally without manipulation

🎯 No Manipulation

Avoid guilt, urgency, or hidden costs

🚪 Easy Unsubscribe

Cancel with the same ease as signing up

❤️ Respect Users

Prioritize user autonomy over business metrics
